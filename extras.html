Plead the .45th - Animated Music Video

This project is an animated music video / lyric video to Plead the .45th by Smino and Saba, for the soundtrack of the movie Judas and the Black Messiah. In Spring 2024, I took an introductury class in 2D animation where I learned the principles of animating, elements of storytelling and how to create simple animations using Adobe AfterEffects and Adobe Premiere. This video was produced for my final project in the class and combined my interests of music and film directing, as well as teaching animation and helping me hone my editing skills.

I do not own the rights to the music in the above video.

Oscillation FM - Weekly Radio Show

>

This project / side hobby formed after I got involved with my school's radio station, WVUM. I found out that I could host my own radio show and play sets that I could curate, with some minor restrictions. Thus, Oscillation was born. I describe Oscillation as a celebration of historically marginalized artists who've contributed insurmountable influence to modern day music, primarily through the genres of soul, r&b, and hip-hop. These genres and their respective subgenres are underlying themes in all modern popular music, so I use oscillation as a way to pay homage to these genres and play lesser known artists who contribute to them. Want to listen? Check out my show on every other Monday from 7-8 here!
