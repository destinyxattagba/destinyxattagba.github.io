Drink Thermometer with LED

>

This semester, I was lucky enough to take a class called Physical Computing, where I was able to use Python, Circuitpython, microboards, and other electrical components to prototype functional ideas, both practical and creative. For this project, I used Circuit Python and an Adafruit Circuit Playground Express board to create a device that holds a beverage and uses a temperature probe to read the temperature of the beverage. Additionally, the device flashes color depending on what the temperature is.

Circuit Python, 3D Printing, Onshape Completed Documentation

Record Player Replica & Audio Movie

>

In my Physical Computing class, I had the creative freedom to execute an Idea that interested me for our final project. I decided to create a replica of a record player that detected the color of "vinyls" (replicas) and played specific mp3 files, telling a story. I told a short story of black hair and the black experience using pop culture clips, interviews, and news recordings.

Circuit Python, 3D Printing, Audacity, Onshape In Progress Documentation
